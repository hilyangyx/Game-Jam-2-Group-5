# Dead Man's Hand

> **This is not a gamble that can be won through luck alone.**

You are caught cheating in the Dealer's casino. According to the rules of the house, there is only one punishment: death. But the Dealer is a gambler. He gives you one final chance: survive three rounds of his deadly games, and you may walk
away.
